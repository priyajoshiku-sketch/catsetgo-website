Hello, I'm Priya

Cats have always been my people

I've been a foster mum to cats for most of my adult life, rescuing, fostering, and helping nervous and injured cats find their feet since I was a student. You could say I found my purr-pose looking after them.

With close to six years of experience across many breeds, I'm comfortable with the shy ones, the skittish ones, and the ones who need a little medical care. I love the patience of helping a scared cat come out of its shell.

People often think cats are aloof and independent, but I've found the opposite: they're gentle, full of character, and they get lonely. They really do notice when their person is away, and that's why I do this. Your cat gets genuine company and comfort at home while you're gone, not just a food bowl being refilled.

I'm fully insured and DBS-checked, and I treat every home and every cat as if they were my own.

Good to know

Questions, answered

What areas do you cover?

I'm based in Fulham and Hammersmith, and visit across West London: Parsons Green, Brook Green, Shepherd's Bush, West Kensington, Barons Court, Chiswick, Chelsea, Kensington, Putney, and Barnes. If you're nearby but not listed, just ask.

Are you insured and background-checked?

Yes. Cat Set Go is fully insured, and I'm DBS-checked, so you can leave your keys and your cat with complete peace of mind.

Can you give medication?

Yes. I'm experienced with giving medication and caring for senior or special-needs cats. A small supplement applies for medication visits, and we'll go through everything at the meet & greet.

How do the updates work?

After each visit I'll send you an update, with photos, a quick note, or whatever you'd prefer, so you're never left wondering. Just let me know what works for you.

What's a meet & greet?

It's a free, no-obligation visit before you book. I come to your home, meet your cat, and we run through feeding, routines, and keys, so everyone's comfortable before your trip.

How do I pay?

Once your booking's confirmed, I'll send you simple payment details. There's nothing to sort out until then.
